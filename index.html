Career Arc

From Pusa to Wageningen.

My work traces a single thread across two decades: understanding how quality seed reaches the farmers who need it most, and removing the barriers — agronomic, institutional, and informational — that stand in the way.

I began with seed physiology at IARI New Delhi (where the Green Revolution's seeds were once developed), moved through the private sector with Incotec, joined Bioversity International for fieldwork in the Indo-Gangetic plains, and have been at Wageningen since 2019, now leading AI and digital innovation for WSER's seed systems portfolio.
