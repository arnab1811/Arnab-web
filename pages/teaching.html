Hands-on training in digital seed traceability systems, mobile data collection, and AI-assisted data analysis tools for government seed inspectors, NGO staff, and seed company managers.

Internal WUR training sessions on responsible GenAI use — including Ollama local model deployment, GDPR compliance for AI-assisted research, and EU AI Act implications for social scientists.

ISTA-compliant seed testing methodology training for national seed testing laboratories in Sub-Saharan Africa — germination testing, seed health, moisture content, and variety identity.

Training in tricot (triadic comparison of technologies) methodology for on-farm variety evaluation with farmer groups — covering trial design, data collection, and analysis with ClimMob.
