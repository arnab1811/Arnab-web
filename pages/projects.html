Digital Seed Systems

Portals, registries & mobile tools.

🇲🇲
Myanmar

Myanmar Seed Portal

National seed variety registry and seed intelligence mobile application for the Myanmar seed sector. Developed under the RSSD/ISSD programme, covering seed stock tracking, logistics, and quality reporting.

🇺🇬
Uganda

Uganda Seed Traceability & Tracking System (STTS)

Mobile-enabled seed lot traceability system for certified seed producers in Uganda. Tracks seed movement from breeder through certified producers to agro-dealers.

🇳🇬
Nigeria

Nigeria Variety Release Portal

Digital platform supporting Nigeria's formal variety registration and release process — reducing bureaucratic lag and increasing transparency for private seed companies and NARS breeders.

🇪🇬
Egypt

Egypt Seed Regulatory Portal

Digitalisation of Egypt's seed regulatory framework — supporting the transition from paper-based to digital variety registration, certification, and seed lot tracking processes.

🏦
Horn of Africa

Community Seed Banks — Horn of Africa

Establishment and support of community seed banks in Somaliland, South Sudan, and Sudan under ISSD Africa. Covers site selection, governance protocols, seed loan systems, and emergency seed security.

🇮🇳
India

India's First Low-Energy Genebank

Co-led development of a desiccated-ambient storage genebank using molecular sieves at ICAR-IIVR Varanasi (with Bioversity International and Prem Mathur). Dramatically lower energy use vs. conventional cold storage.
